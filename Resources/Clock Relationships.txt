Secret number is based off of relationship between the angles the minute and hour hand make on a clock, with a defined 0/start value
  (any position on the clock to make it harder) 



Test Time: 3:01 (http://www.visnos.com/demos/clock)

Minute * 6 = Degrees of the minute hand past 0
Minute / 60 = Percentage of how far the hour hand should be past its spot(HRP%)
HRP% * 360 = Degrees hour hand is past its spot

Hour Hand Spot * 30 = Hour hand spot degrees
